Welcome

We understand that the loss of a loved one can be overwhelming. Our role is to ease that burden by managing every aspect of the funeral process with sensitivity, efficiency, and cultural respect.

From body pickup to burial or cremation, we handle all arrangements so families can focus on grieving and honouring the life of their loved one with peace of mind.

About Us

Gcilishe Taliwe Classic Funerals is a proudly South African, fully woman-owned funeral services business established in 2010. We are BBBEE compliant and deeply committed to serving families with dignity, respect, and professionalism.
